# Privacy Policy for Rota

**Last updated: November 7, 2025**

## Overview

Rota ("we", "our", or "us") is a meal planning application that helps households manage their meal rotations.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Permission
We request camera permission solely to scan QR codes for joining households. We do not store, transmit, or process any photos or camera data. The camera is only used for real-time QR code scanning.

### User Data
- Anonymous authentication ID (provided by Firebase)
- Household membership information
- Meal preferences and rotation data

All user data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and is only accessible to members of your household.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Camera Access**: Only used to scan QR codes for household invitations
- **User Data**: Used to provide the meal rotation functionality and sync data across devices in your household

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data remains private within your household.

## Data Security

All data is stored using Firebase's secure infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.

## Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by leaving your household through the app settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: apps@egl.is
